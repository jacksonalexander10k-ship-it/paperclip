# Aygency World — Demo Presentation Script

**Duration:** 8-10 minutes
**Audience:** Agency owners, real estate entrepreneurs, investors
**Setup:** Run `pnpm --filter @paperclipai/db exec tsx src/seed-demo.ts` before the demo. Log into the demo company "Dubai Prestige Properties."

---

>
> That's Aygency World. Let me show you what it looks like when you wake up in the morning."

**Screen: CEO Chat**

> "This is your CEO Chat. You talk to one agent — your CEO, Khalid. He manages everything else."

Scroll to the morning brief message. Read highlights:

**Screen: CEO Chat / Approvals**

   > "Layla drafted this in formal Arabic with the correct greeting — 'Dear Mr. Al Hashimi.' Two weeks ago she was writing 'Hi Ahmed! 😊'. I corrected her once. She learned."

   > "Notice this one. Russian lead. Layla leads with the price drop percentage, ROI, and exact AED figure. No pleasantries. Russians want numbers. Layla learned that from my corrections too."

3. **Instagram post (Creek Harbour launch)**
   > "Nour prepared this based on Omar's market alert. Every post now includes: project name, type, price, payment plan, and one differentiating detail. Because I edited her posts to include that."

   > "This is a closing message. Developer confirmed a 5% cash discount. Dmitri said he's ready to sign this week. One click to send."

**Demo the edit-before-approve:** Click edit on one card, change a word, approve.

> "I can edit any message before it goes out. And that edit becomes a learning — next time Layla will write it my way."

## Q&A Prep — Common Questions

**"Can I control what goes out?"**
Yes. Every external action requires your approval. You can edit any message before it's sent. You set auto-approve rules for routine follow-ups.

**"How long to set up?"**
Same day. Sign up, connect WhatsApp and Gmail, answer your CEO's questions. First leads handled within hours.

**"What about my existing team?"**
Aygency World handles the back-office — leads, content, market intel. Your human brokers focus on viewings and closing deals. AI handles volume, humans handle relationships.

**"What languages?"**
Arabic, English, Russian out of the box. Agents auto-detect and respond in the lead's language.

**"How much does it cost?"**
Starting from AED 999/month for CEO + 2 agents. Less than one broker's daily salary per month.

**"Is my data safe?"**
Every agency is completely isolated. Your agents can't see another agency's data. All credentials encrypted at rest.

**"What if an agent makes a mistake?"**
Nothing goes out without your approval. If an agent's output is wrong, reject or edit it — the agent learns and won't make the same mistake again.
