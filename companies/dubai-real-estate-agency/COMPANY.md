Dubai Real Estate Agency is a fully AI-operated real estate business focused on Dubai's off-plan and secondary market. The agency owner interacts exclusively with the CEO agent, who coordinates all sub-agents and reports back with summaries, approvals, and escalations.

The agency operates 24/7. Agents run on heartbeats — continuously, without being prompted. The owner's job is to review approvals, give strategic direction, and close deals.

## Org Chart

```
Owner (Board)
  └── CEO
        └── Lead Agent (Layla)
```

## Focus Areas

- Off-plan projects: JVC, Sports City, Dubai Marina, Creek Harbour, Business Bay, Downtown, Palm Jumeirah
- Secondary sales and rentals across all major areas
- International buyer segments: Arabic, Russian, Chinese, British, Indian
- Investor-grade CMAs and pitch decks

## Operating Standards

- Every inbound lead gets a response within 5 minutes
- All outbound WhatsApp/email requires owner approval before sending
- RERA compliance on all communications — no guaranteed yields, no misleading price claims
- Language matching — respond in the same language the lead used
