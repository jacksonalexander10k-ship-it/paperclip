## Payment Plan Structures (Off-Plan)

- **40/60**: 40% during construction, 60% on handover. Most common.
- **60/40**: 60% during construction, 40% post-handover over 2–3 years.
- **1% monthly**: Popular with budget buyers — pay 1% per month throughout construction.
- **Post-handover**: Some developers offer 3–5 year post-handover plans. High demand.

## Golden Visa

- AED 2M+ property investment qualifies for 10-year UAE residency Golden Visa.
- Must be completed (ready) property or off-plan from approved developer.
- Covers investor + spouse + children.
- No minimum stay requirement to maintain.
- Always mention this to international investors with budget > AED 2M.

## DLD Fees and Transaction Costs

- **DLD Transfer Fee**: 4% of purchase price (paid by buyer)
- **Admin fee**: AED 4,200 (apartment) / AED 4,000 (villa)
- **Trustee fee**: AED 4,000 (under AED 500K) / AED 8,000 (over AED 500K)
- **Mortgage registration** (if financed): 0.25% of loan amount
- **Agency commission**: 2% (standard for secondary sales)
- Total buyer costs: ~4.5–5% on top of purchase price

## Rental Market

- **Gross yields**: JVC 6–8%, Marina 5–7%, Downtown 4–6%, Palm 3–5%
- **Ejari**: Mandatory tenancy registration with RERA. AED 220 fee.
- **RERA rental index**: Maximum rent increase capped by RERA calculator (based on market rates).
- **Leases**: Annual cheques (1–4 cheques standard). Monthly payments rare.
- **Deposits**: 5% security deposit (furnished/unfurnished).

## Market Dynamics (2026)

- Strong demand from European and GCC buyers
- Off-plan launches consistently oversubscribed at premium price points
- Secondary market: price discovery ongoing post-2023 peak in some areas
- Rental market: vacancy at historic lows in prime areas
- Supply: large pipeline in affordable segment (JVC, Arjan, Dubailand)
