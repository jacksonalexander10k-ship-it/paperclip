# Dubai Compliance Rules

## RERA Advertising Rules

### What You MUST Include

- RERA permit number on any property advertisement
- Broker card number (ORN) for any agent-attributed communication
- "Prices subject to change" on any price quote
- Project name and developer name (accurate, no abbreviations that mislead)

### What You MUST NEVER Say

- **"Guaranteed rental yield of X%"** — illegal. RERA prohibits yield guarantees.
- **"Capital appreciation guaranteed"** — illegal.
- **"Price going up next week"** — pressure tactics banned by RERA.
- **"Only 2 units left"** — false scarcity claims are a violation.
- **"100% finance available"** — mortgage LTV is capped by UAE Central Bank (50–75%).
- **"Residency guaranteed with purchase"** — Golden Visa eligibility is subject to RERA/ICP conditions, not guaranteed by the developer.

### Safe Alternatives

Instead of yield guarantees:
> "Historical rental yields in this area have averaged X% based on DLD data. Past performance doesn't guarantee future returns."

Instead of price pressure:
> "Prices are subject to developer confirmation. I'd recommend acting when you're comfortable."

Instead of false scarcity:
> "I'll check current availability with the developer and confirm."

## PDPA (Personal Data Protection)

- Only collect data necessary for the service requested.
- Never share lead data with third parties without consent.
- On request, confirm you can delete their data.
- Do not store passport copies, visa copies, or financial documents unless explicitly requested and stored securely.

## Financial Disclaimer

Always include when discussing investment returns, yields, or ROI projections:

> "These figures are based on current market data and are for informational purposes only. They do not constitute financial advice or a guarantee of returns."

## Communication Rules

- No WhatsApp/email/SMS to a lead who has asked to stop receiving communications.
- No bulk messages to purchased lead lists (PDPA violation).
- Always identify the agency name in communications.
- No misleading "before" prices in promotional materials.

## Off-Plan Specific

- Off-plan sales must be from RERA-registered projects with escrow accounts.
- Buyers must receive a Sale Purchase Agreement (SPA) — never accept cash without SPA.
- Project must have a valid RERA NOC (No Objection Certificate) before marketing.
- Developer must provide RERA project registration number on all materials.

## What to Do If a Lead Asks About Returns

Provide data, not guarantees:
1. Share DLD transaction history for the building/area (use search_dld_transactions tool).
2. Share current Bayut/Dubizzle rental listings as reference (use search_listings tool).
3. Add the financial disclaimer.
4. Recommend they consult a financial advisor for investment decisions.
