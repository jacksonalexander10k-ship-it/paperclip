# Rent Collection — Dubai Real Estate

## Cheque Management

### Standard Payment Frequencies
| Cheques/Year | Common For | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Premium tenants, corporate leases | Best for landlord cash flow |
| 2 | Standard apartments | Most common in Dubai |
| 4 | Mid-range tenants | Quarterly payments |
| 12 | Short-term, flexible | Higher admin overhead |

### Cheque Processing
1. Collect post-dated cheques at lease signing
2. Log each cheque: number, amount, date, bank
3. Deposit cheque on or after due date
4. Allow 3-5 business days for clearing
5. Update status: deposited → cleared

### Bounced Cheque Protocol
1. Contact tenant immediately (WhatsApp + call)
2. Give 48-hour grace period for replacement cheque
3. If not resolved: formal written notice
4. If still unresolved after 7 days: escalate to landlord + CEO
5. Legal options: police complaint (bounced cheque is a criminal offence in UAE), RDC filing
6. Note: as of 2022, partial decriminalisation — civil route preferred for amounts < AED 200K

## Arrears Management
- Flag any cheque overdue > 3 days
- Escalate overdue > 7 days to CEO
- Overdue > 30 days: recommend legal notice
- Track total arrears per landlord for monthly reporting

## Landlord Settlement
- Monthly or quarterly (per management agreement)
- Statement includes:
  - Gross rent collected (cleared cheques only)
  - Less: management fee (typically 5-10%)
  - Less: maintenance costs (with receipts)
  - Less: any withholding for future maintenance reserve
  - Net payout to landlord
- Transfer net amount within 5 business days of statement
- Retain copies for 5 years (audit requirement)
