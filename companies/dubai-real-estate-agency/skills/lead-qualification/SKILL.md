# Lead Qualification

## Goal

Qualify every lead over 2–3 messages. Collect enough to assign a score 0–10 and decide next action.

## The 5 Qualification Dimensions

Collect these in natural conversation — do not send them as a list:

1. **Budget** — What range? Cash or mortgage?
2. **Timeline** — When do they want to move/invest? Under 6 months, 6–12 months, 12+ months?
3. **Area preference** — Specific area or open?
4. **Purpose** — End use (own home) or investment?
5. **Financing** — Cash buyer, pre-approved mortgage, or exploring?

## Scoring Rubric

Start at 0. Add points:

| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Specific area mentioned | +1 |
| Budget range given | +2 |
| Timeline < 6 months | +2 |
| Cash buyer or mortgage pre-approved | +2 |
| Prior property purchase in Dubai | +1 |
| Referred by existing client | +1 |
| High-budget (> AED 3M) | +1 |
| Vague / no details after 2 exchanges | −1 |
| Unresponsive for 24+ hours | −1 |

## Score Interpretation

| Score | Label | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| 8–10 | Hot | Escalate to CEO immediately. Propose viewing within 24 hours. |
| 5–7 | Warm | Continue qualification. Aim to book viewing within 1 week. |
| 2–4 | Cool | Enrol in 30-day nurture sequence. Light follow-up. |
| 0–1 | Cold | Log and park. Re-engage at 90 days. |

## Question Sequencing

Ask one question per message. Do not interrogate.

**Round 1 (first response):** Pick whichever is most missing — area OR budget.

**Round 2 (after they reply):** Ask timeline or purpose.

**Round 3 (if needed):** Ask financing status.

By round 3 you should have enough to score. If not, score conservatively and flag to CEO.

## When to Stop Qualifying

- Score ≥ 8: Stop asking questions. Propose a viewing.
- Lead asks for specific property details: Provide them, then follow up with qualification question.
- Lead is unresponsive after 2 messages: Enrol in nurture sequence.
- Lead explicitly says "just browsing": Score 2, enrol in 90-day nurture.

## Dubai-Specific Qualification Notes

- **Golden Visa eligibility**: AED 2M+ investment in real estate. Mention if they're asking about residency.
- **Off-plan vs ready**: Off-plan buyers usually want payment plans. Ask early.
- **Payment plans**: Most Dubai off-plan is 40/60 or 60/40 (% during construction / on handover). Mention if budget-sensitive.
- **DLD fees**: 4% transfer fee on purchase. Always factor into total budget.
- **Mortgage**: Non-residents can get UAE mortgages (up to 50% LTV). Residents up to 75%.
