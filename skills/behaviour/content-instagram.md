# Instagram Content Skill

## Content Pillars (rotate daily)

| Day | Pillar | Example |
|-----|--------|---------|
| Sunday | Project showcase | New launch spotlight, project walkthrough |
| Monday | Market insight | Weekly market stat, price movement, DLD data |
| Tuesday | Lifestyle / Area guide | "Living in Dubai Marina — what to expect" |
| Wednesday | Investment tip | ROI breakdown, payment plan explainer, Golden Visa guide |
| Thursday | Client success / Testimonial | "Just handed over keys", deal story (anonymized) |
| Friday | Community / Behind the scenes | Team photo, office tour, Friday market visit |
| Saturday | Listing spotlight | Featured property with details |

## Posting Schedule
- 1 post per day minimum. 2 for active agencies.
- Best times for Dubai audience: 12:00pm, 6:00pm, 9:00pm (Dubai time).
- Stories: 3-5 per day (less polished, more authentic).
- Reels: 2-3 per week (short property tours, market tips, area guides).

## Carousel Structure (Main Post Format)

### Slide Layout (8-10 slides)
1. **Hook slide**: Bold statement or question that stops the scroll. Large text, minimal design.
   - "JVC just hit AED 1,400/sqft. Here's why."
   - "5 reasons Dubai Marina beats Downtown for investors"
   - "AED 2M gets you THIS in Palm Jumeirah"
2. **Context slide**: Set the scene. One paragraph max.
3. **Value slides (3-4)**: One key point per slide. Use numbers, comparisons, charts.
4. **Data slide**: A comparison table, price chart, or ROI calculation.
5. **CTA slide**: Clear call to action. "DM us for the full payment plan" or "Save this for later."

### Design Rules
- Clean, professional. Not overly designed — authenticity wins on Instagram.
- Use the agency's brand colors consistently.
- High-quality renders for project showcases (from developer materials).
- Text must be readable on mobile (minimum 24pt equivalent).
- No stock photos of random skylines — use actual project renders or real photos.

## Caption Formula

```
[Hook — first line that appears before "...more"]

[Value — 2-3 sentences of insight, data, or story]

[CTA — what should the reader do?]

[Hashtags — in a comment or after line breaks]
```

### Example Caption
```
Dubai Marina apartments just crossed AED 2,800/sqft average. Here's what that means for investors.

In Q1 2026, Marina saw a 12% increase in transaction volume compared to Q4 2025. Rental yields remain strong at 5.5-6.5% gross, and with the new Bluewaters bridge, walkability has never been better.

#DubaiRealEstate #DubaiMarina #DubaiInvestment #PropertyInvestment #DubaiProperty #OffPlanDubai #GoldenVisa #DubaiLife
```

## Hashtag Strategy

### Mix per post (15-20 hashtags)
- **High volume (5-7)**: #DubaiRealEstate #DubaiProperty #DubaiLife #UAE #Dubai #LuxuryRealEstate #PropertyInvestment
- **Mid volume (5-7)**: #DubaiMarina #OffPlanDubai #DubaiInvestor #GoldenVisaDubai #DubaiHills #BusinessBay
- **Niche (3-5)**: #JVCDubai #EmaarProperties #SobhaHartland #DubaiCreekHarbour #DubaiBroker
- **Branded (1-2)**: #[AgencyName] #[AgencyTagline]

### Rules
- Place hashtags in the first comment, not the caption body (cleaner look).
- Rotate hashtag sets — do not use the exact same set every post (Instagram penalizes this).
- Research trending Dubai real estate hashtags monthly.

## Content Types to Generate

### Project Showcase Post
- 8-slide carousel: hero render, key stats (price, size, completion), payment plan, amenities, location map, CTA.
- Caption: project name, developer, starting price, one unique selling point, CTA.

### Market Insight Post
- Data-driven. Pull from DLD transactions, RERA data, or news.
- Format: stat + context + what it means for buyers/investors.

### Area Guide Post
- "Everything you need to know about [Area]" format.
- Include: lifestyle, price range, best projects, who lives there, pros/cons.

### Investment Tip Post
- Educational. "How to calculate ROI on Dubai property" or "Golden Visa: the complete guide."
- Position the agency as an expert, not a salesperson.

## Approval Flow
- All Instagram posts must go through the CEO Chat approval queue before publishing.
- The approval card shows: image preview, caption text, hashtags, suggested posting time.
- Owner can edit the caption before approving.
- Auto-approve can be configured: "queue posts for review but auto-publish if not reviewed within 4 hours."
